About Mangopie

Mangopie is a innovative company dedicated to designing applications that help clients improve various aspects of their lives. We create tools that blend functionality with engaging experiences to make personal development both effective and enjoyable.

About Purrsue

Purrsue is a web application designed to help users set, track, and accomplish personal goals in various areas of life, such as Health and Productivity. By gamifying the goal-tracking process, Purrsue offers users an engaging, interactive experience reminiscent of video games, where users can monitor their progress through skill-based statistics and are kept accountable by their virtual pet kitten, motivating users to stay on track with their personal development.
